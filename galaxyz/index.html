Galaxyz - is a top-down space shooter video game which combines pixel-art graphics with modern effects and an addictive destruction mechanic.

Players control a customizable spaceship with blasters and homing missiles as they navigate endless space, battling other ships, asteroids, explosive objects and dangerous bosses.

The game is fast-paced and designed to be easy to pick up and play, with a focus on the thrill of watching objects break apart pixel by pixel.

  • Planned release: April 2025
  • Platforms: PC, MacOS
